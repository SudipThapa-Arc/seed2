# 🎨 World Labs Design Tokens

## Colors (Modern AI Aesthetic)
- **Background (Primary):** `hsl(0 0% 0%)` (Deep Black)
- **Surface (Cards/Bento):** `hsl(0 0% 5%)` (Charcoal/Dark Gray)
- **Foreground (Primary Text):** `hsl(0 0% 100%)` (Pure White)
- **Foreground (Muted Text):** `hsl(0 0% 65%)` (Light Gray)
- **Accent (Gradients):** Subtle vibrant gradients for hover states and key highlights.
- **Border:** `hsl(0 0% 15%)` (Subtle boundary lines)

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter or Roobert (Geometrical, clean sans-serif)
- **Hero Title:** `text-6xl` to `text-8xl`, `font-bold`, tight tracking (`tracking-tighter`).
- **Section Headers:** `text-3xl` to `text-5xl`, `font-semibold`.
- **Body:** `text-lg` or `text-base`, relaxed line height (`leading-relaxed`).

## Spacing & Layout
- **Hero Section Padding:** `py-32` to `py-48`
- **Section Gap:** `gap-y-24` or `gap-y-32`
- **Bento Grid Gap:** `gap-6` or `gap-8`
- **Border Radius:** `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl` for bento cards, emphasizing modern spatial feel.
